Gotta Catch 'Em All

Pokemon Text Based A Totally Original Game, 2022
Pokemon Text Based A Totally Original Game was my final for Intro to Python. I have always loved Pokemon Games and this was a fun little game that also got me into the work I was doing for class. In this game you start off like any pokemon game does, you pick your starter, battle your rival and head out on your own. After leaving your town you are able to go to 4 areas. The Wild and battle rng trainers or battle rng pokemon which you can catch. The Pokemon Center to heal your Pokemon. The Pokemart to buy Potions and Pokeballs. And lastly the Pokemon Gym. Which you must be level 10 to enter.
